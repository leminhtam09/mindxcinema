Space Cadet

Tiffany "Rex" Simpson has always dreamed of going to space, and her "doctored" application lands her in NASA's ultra-competitive astronaut training program. In over her head, can this Florida girl rely on her quick wits, moxie, and determination to make it through the program and prove everyone wrong? A heartwarming and hilarious journey of self-discovery and ambition.
